    <p>
      Welcome! I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Political Science at the
      University of California, Davis. My research focuses on parties, elections,
      and representation, primarily in advanced industrial democracies. I am 
      particularly focused on how parties as institutions have their behavior shaped
      by both internal characteristics, such as ties to civil society groups or the
      ideological beliefs of their politicians, and external characteristics such as
      the dilemmas of multiparty competition or campaign finance laws, and how these
      aspects affect representation. I also have current ongoing work involving
      representation in the World Health Assembly and US house elections.
    </p>
